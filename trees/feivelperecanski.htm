Feivel Perecanski

From left to right, Sheine Michle (Perecanski) Rabinowich, Chana Perecanski, Feivel Perecanski, Chana’s daughter Feige Lea, and Lea (Perecanski) Elhanani.

This picture, from the mid-1930s, shows Lea (Perecanski) Elhanani’s family: Lea is standing in the middle and, from left to right in the front row, are her sister Tamara, her mother Rivka, her father Feivel and her brother Josef.
